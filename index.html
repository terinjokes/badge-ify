Badge-ify

Let your site's users know that your site is protected by CloudFlare, in the most annoying manner possible.

By showing them a badge, on every page load!

Bugs

Badge-ify currently does not support "flash cookies" to remember if the user has visited the site before, and to not show the badge. This is because we are currently unaware of any alternatives methods to keep track of users that is also painfully difficult for them to remove. If you know of one, comment on issue #1.
